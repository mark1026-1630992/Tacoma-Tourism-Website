Museum of Glass

The Museum of Glass features glass artworks from various artists. Most prominently featured, are the works of Dale Chihuly! They have an indoor exhibit of Chihuly's work
as well as an out outdoor exhibit on the bridge of glass where you can see them prominently displayed in glass cases as well as above you The museum also features a glass workshop
called "The Hot Shop" where you can watch the artists in action. After watching some live glass-blowing, you can grab some refreshments and a bite to eat at the cafe.

For More Information Visit: Musuem of Glass


Washington State History Museum

Come learn about various aspects of Washington States history! They have various exhibits on display that change every
few months You can learn about many things, from Mt Saint Helens erupting, to the collapse of galloping gertie.
You can also lean a lot aboout the geography of Washington

For More Information Visit: Washington State History Museum


Tacoma Art Museum

The Tacoma Art Museum has something for the whole family. They have a cafe where you can grab a bite to eat.
TAM also has an art studio where people of all ages can express their creativity
And when You're done exploring the museum, you can take a little piece home with you from the gift shop.
For More Information Visit: Tacoma Art Museum
