Point Ruston Historic Ferry

Come see the historic Point Ruston Ferry!

Ruston Spray park

Children will have lots of fun splashing around in the spray park.

Chinese Reconciliation Park

This park was founded to honor the memory of the chinese people who were forced out of Tacoma in 1885. Enjoy a view of the water as well as a relaxing park and garden.

Century Theatres

Come enjoy a nice film at Century Theatres.

Point Ruston Waterwalk

Enjoy over one mile stretch to walk, skate, rollerblade, or jog. Watch the waves and exercise!



Lodging

Silver Cloud Hotel

After a day of fun, you can Spend the night at the lovely Silver Cloud Hotel. Wake up to the sounds of crashing waves and begin another day of fun.
