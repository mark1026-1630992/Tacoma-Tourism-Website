1. Silver Cloud Inn

Located in Tacoma’s Old Town community is the Silver Cloud Inn Tacoma Waterfront, Tacoma’s only waterfront hotel.
Our hotel is conveniently located just two miles north of downtown Tacoma’s amazing museum district, and is only ten minutes from Tacoma Dome & Convention Center.

2317 Ruston Way, Tacoma, WA 98402 | 253.272.1300

2. The Geiger Bed and Breakfast

The Geiger Victorian Bed and Breakfast is located in the North Slope Historic District close to downtown, Tacoma Dome, Museum
of Glass, Convention Center, Tacoma General Hospital, Mary Bridge Hospital, St. Joseph Hospital, University of Puget Sound, waterfront restaurants
and Annie Wright School. Easy access to Interstate 5 and Olympic peninsula.

912 N I St, Tacoma, WA 98403 | 253.383.3504

3. The Villa Bed and Breakfast

A luxurious Tacoma Bed & Breakfast, owners Toni and Martin invite you to taste a bit of old-world Italy
Tacoma B and B, Washington Bed and Breakfast, Seattle Bed and Breakfast

705 North 5th Street Tacoma WA 98403 | 253.572.1157

4. Hotel Murano

Welcome to Hotel Murano, an artful downtown Tacoma hotel located steps from the Tacoma Convention Center and two to three blocks from the
city’s best museums and restaurants. In honor of Tacoma’s place in the American glass art movement, Hotel Murano pairs luxurious guestrooms and
well-appointed event space with a museum-worthy collection of glass art from around the world.

1320 Broadway Tacoma, WA 98402 | 253.238.8000
